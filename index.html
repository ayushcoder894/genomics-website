FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of tests do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of genetic and diagnostic tests including DNA Wellness Testing, Paternity Testing, STR Analysis, Prenatal Testing, and various other specialized genomic tests. Visit our services page for a complete list.

How long does it take to get test results?

Turnaround time varies by test type. Most routine tests take 3-5 business days, while specialized genetic tests may take 7-14 days. We'll provide an estimated timeframe when you book your test.

Are your tests accurate and reliable?

Yes, we maintain a 99.9% accuracy rate. Our lab is accredited by the United Kingdom Accreditation Forum (UKAF) and follows strict quality control protocols. All tests are performed by experienced professionals using state-of-the-art equipment.

Do you offer home sample collection?

Yes, we provide home sample collection services for your convenience. Additional charges may apply based on your location. Contact us to schedule a home visit.

What are the payment options available?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI, and online bank transfers. We also offer partner pricing for multiple tests and installment options for expensive tests.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. We follow strict privacy protocols and all patient information is kept 100% confidential. Your test results are only shared with you through secure channels.
